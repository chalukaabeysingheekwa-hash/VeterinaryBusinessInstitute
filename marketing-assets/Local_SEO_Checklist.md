# VBI Local SEO & Google Business Profile (GBP) Checklist

While your veterinary website's codebase handles the technical on-page Local SEO signals, your Google Business Profile (GBP) is an external Google property that manages how you appear on Google Maps and the "Local Pack" (the 3 map results at the top of a search).

## 1. What Your New Website is Already Doing
We have hardcoded the following Local SEO signals directly into your Next.js website architecture:
- **Schema.org structured data**: We injected a `PostalAddress` object into your header that explicitly parses `303, Pinetree Way Mississauga, Ontario L5G 2R4 Canada` alongside your business name and `(833) 523-1845` phone number. This perfectly structures your **NAP** (Name, Address, Phone) data for Google's crawlers.
- **Location Context**: Your Footer and Contact pages surface this geographic data natively. 

## 2. Google Business Profile Optimization Checklist
To maximize your Local SEO, ensure your actual Google Maps listing (GBP) follows these rules:

- [ ] **Exact NAP Consistency**: Ensure that the name, address, and phone number in your Google Business Profile *exactly* matches the one we put on the website (`303, Pinetree Way Mississauga...`).
- [ ] **Primary Category**: Set your primary category to `Marketing Consultant` or `Business Management Consultant` (or `Veterinarian` for your clients).
- [ ] **Link to Your New Site**: Ensure the "Website" button on your GBP links directly to `https://www.veterinarybusinessinstitute.com`.
- [ ] **High-Quality Photos**: Upload photos of your team, office, or event stages to signal a legitimate business presence.
- [ ] **Review Generation**: The number and velocity of Google Reviews is the #1 ranking factor for the Local Pack. Consistently ask your successful coaching clients to leave a 5-star review specifically mentioning your services.
- [ ] **Regular Posts**: Use the Google Business "Updates/Offers" feature to cross-post links to your new VBI Podcast episodes or Webinar Replays directly onto your Google map listing.

By pairing your newly built, Schema-optimized Next.js website with an active Google Business Profile, you establish maximum local authority.
